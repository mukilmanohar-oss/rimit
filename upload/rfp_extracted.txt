     1. Executive Summary
   RIMIT Education, operating in conjunction with SPES Education under the RIMIT Educational Charitable
   Trust, is inviting proposals from established software development agencies to build a robust, scalable,
   cloud-based Business-to-Business (B2B) Educational Portal and Centralized Admission Management
   System.

   The objective of this project is twofold. First, it will serve as a national University Aggregator, bringing
   diverse universities across India under a single digital umbrella where information regarding courses,
   dynamic fee structures, eligibility criteria, and digital prospectuses is systematically structured. Second, it
   will function as an operational B2B Portal empowering our vast network of sub-centers and partner
   institutions nationwide to directly register students, manage demographic and academic profiles, and
   securely upload required admission documents.


     2. Company Background & Operational Context
   RIMIT Education is a premier educational consultancy and distance education administration
   organization based in Kerala. Our critical roles include acting as the National Coordinator for Kerala for
   Mangalayatan University (Online/ODL streams) and serving as the Regional Center Coordinator in
   Kerala for the Board of Open Schooling and Skill Education (BOSSE).

   Our daily operations involve managing extensive student recruitment pipelines across decentralized sub-
   centers. Currently, partner institutions suffer from fragmented data spread across disjointed university
   channels. This platform will unify these offerings, optimizing administrative productivity and accuracy.

   The selected vendor will deliver an end-to-end cloud platform encompassing the following core
   architectural modules:

Module 1: Centralized University Aggregator Hub

 • Unified University Directory: Comprehensive profiles for all partner universities across India,
   managed dynamically via a central administrative panel.
 • Course & Fee Repository: Exhaustive listing of courses (Undergraduate, Postgraduate, Diploma,
   Open Schooling) searchable by stream, eligibility, duration, and structured fees.
 • Prospectus & Document Library: A centralized digital vault allowing instant access, viewing, and

Module 2: B2B Sub-Center Partner & Admission Portal

   Critical Operational Requirement: Sub-centers must be equipped with an intuitive interface to
   handle student enrollments autonomously while adhering to rigid validation protocols set by the
   central management.


 • Direct Student Registration: Authorized sub-centers can log in securely to input candidate details
   including full demographic profiles, contact data, and chosen academic pathways.
 • Document Upload Vault: Seamless drag-and-drop mechanism for sub-centers to upload high-
   resolution applicant documentation (Identity Proofs, Marklists, Migration Certificates, and Experience
   Certificates).
 • Real-time Status Tracking: B2B partners can monitor the exact position of their candidates within
   the multi-stage admission workflow (e.g., Applied, Document Verified, Fee Paid, Enrollment
   Generated).

Module 3: Advanced Business Logic & Intake Controls

 • Session Enforcement Matrix: Programmatic validation rules mapping specific student eligibility to
   distinct intake cycles. For example, the software must programmatically enforce constraints
   preventing fresh candidates from enrolling in restricted sessions (e.g., the July session), automatically
   routing them to subsequent valid terms (e.g., October session).
 • Multi-tier Access Hierarchy: Granular access configuration mapping directly to specific
   organizational roles:
     ◦ Super Admin (MD): Complete operational, financial, and architectural control.
     ◦ Academic Head / Managers: Holistic pipeline supervision and macro-analytical reporting.
     ◦ Academic Counselors / Admission Officers: Localized view limited to directly assigned leads or
       sub-center allocations.
     ◦ Finance / Accounts Officers: Financial modules including ledger tracking, fee confirmations, and
       receipt issuance.

  6. Vendor Proposal Submission Requirements
Responding agencies must structure their submissions cleanly, providing detailed insight into the
following items:

1. Corporate Overview: Executive summary of corporate history, engineering team size, location of
   development centers, and core areas of specialization.
2. Technical Blueprint: Explicit definition of the proposed tech stack (Backend, Frontend, Databases,
   Cloud Infrastructure) detailing how high-concurrency document searches and secure asset streaming
   will be managed.
3. Relevant Case Studies: Documentation of at least two enterprise-scale projects of comparable
   complexity (e.g., B2B aggregator portals, multi-tier CRM systems, or heavy educational management
   ERPs).
4. Commercial Breakdown: Itemized costing covering UI/UX phase, development cycles, cloud setup,
   API integration expenses, and ongoing annual maintenance contracts (AMC).
5. Implementation Schedule: Comprehensive timeline chart detailing development sprints and
   deployment strategy.
